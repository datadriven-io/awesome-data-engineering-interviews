# Contributing

This is a curated list. Quality matters more than quantity.

## Suggesting a new question

Open an issue with the title `[Question] <pattern name>` containing:

1. **The pattern the question tests** - what skill is the interviewer probing?
2. **A real source** - interview report, blog post, or your own first-hand experience.
3. **Why the top 75** - what existing question would you remove to make room?

## Suggesting a structural change

Open an issue describing the change and the motivation. Examples:

- A new section (e.g. "Streaming Systems")
- A reordering of difficulty
- A new field in the question table

## Reporting a broken link

Open an issue with the title `[Broken link] <question name>`.

## Code of conduct

Be kind. This list exists to help people get jobs. Disagreements about
question quality are welcome; personal attacks are not.
